About MLSA

The Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors (MLSA) program is designed to empower students worldwide by fostering leadership, technical skills, and community engagement within the Microsoft ecosystem. Our mission focuses on:

  • Student Leadership: Develop skills to guide and inspire peers.
  • Technology Learning: Access free Microsoft Learn resources and certifications.
  • Community Impact: Organize events and meetups to build local tech communities.
  • Microsoft Ecosystem: Dive deep into Azure, AI, and more with exclusive tools.
